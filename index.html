Biography

I am currently 2nd-year Master student in Institute of Computer Science and Technology of Peking University. My supervisor is Fengyan song and Prof. Dongyan Zhao. Before that, I got my BS degree from Nanjing University, 2014 under the supervision of Prof. Yuming Zhou.

Research Interests

My research interest focuses on information retrieval, machine learning and recommender system. Currently, I mainly work on collaborative filtering, deep learning and their applications in recommender systems.
